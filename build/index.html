Who We Are

We are the Clemson College Republicans—unapologetically conservative, advocating for national and local policy change since our founding. As one of Clemson’s oldest clubs, we serve as a launch pad for political internships and connections. Federated under the College Republican National Committee, our chapter is a vibrant community where young conservatives can engage in meaningful action, from volunteering in local campaigns to hosting influential speakers.
Join us to be part of a network dedicated to shaping the future of conservative politics.
