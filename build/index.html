Who We Are

We are the Clemson College Republicans—unapologetically conservative, advocating for national and local policy change since our founding. As one of Clemson’s oldest clubs, we serve as a launch pad for political internships and connections. Federated under the College Republican National Committee, our chapter is a vibrant community where young conservatives can engage in meaningful action, from volunteering in local campaigns to hosting influential speakers. Join us to be part of a network dedicated to shaping the future of conservative politics.

We believe in
  • Promote the views and values of the Republican Party to the Clemson community.
  • Aid in organizing and supporting the Republican Party at all levels.
  • Foster political and public speaking skills among our members.
