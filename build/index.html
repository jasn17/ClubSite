Our Mission

To promote the views and values of the Republican Party to the Clemson community.

To aid in the organizing and functioning of the Republican party at all levels.

To aid in the Republican Party in elections at all levels of government.

To foster political and public speaking skills among members.

To prepare members for future careers in politics.
